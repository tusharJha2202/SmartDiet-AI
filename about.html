    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does SmartDiet AI create personalized meal plans?
    Our AI analyzes your personal data including age, weight, height, activity level, dietary preferences, allergies, and health goals. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, we create a customized meal plan that's tailored specifically to your needs and preferences.
    Can I modify my diet plan based after Generation?
    Yes! Our platform allows you to easily customize your meal plans. You can swap meals, exclude ingredients you don't like, and adjust portion sizes. The AI learns from your preferences to make better recommendations over time.
    How accurate are the nutritional calculations?
    Our nutritional calculations are based on comprehensive food databases and are accurate to within 5% margin of error. We regularly update our database with the latest nutritional information from verified sources.
    Do you support special dietary requirements?
    Yes! We support various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, keto, paleo, gluten-free, dairy-free, and many others. Our AI can accommodate multiple dietary restrictions simultaneously.
